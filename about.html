About College

Bhavan's College, founded under the aegis of 'BHARTIYA VIDYA BHAVAN' by Kulpati Dr. K.M MUNSHI, is in its 75th year providing an excellent platform to the students for enhancing their overall persona offering higher studies to them and proudly owning a sprawling evergreen campus. This towering institute is located in Mumbai's suburban heart - Andheri! The college's motto 'AMRUTAM TU VIDYA' meaning 'Knowledge is Nectar' is implemented in the most correct manner. 'Buildings can be built overnight but not great institutions!'
