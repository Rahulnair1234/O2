About College

Bhavan's College, founded under the aegis of 'BHARTIYA VIDYA BHAVAN' by Kulpati Dr. K.M MUNSHI, is in its 75th year providing an excellent platform to the students for enhancing their overall persona offering higher studies to them and proudly owning a sprawling evergreen campus. This towering institute is located in Mumbai's suburban heart - Andheri! The college's motto 'AMRUTAM TU VIDYA' meaning 'Knowledge is Nectar' is implemented in the most correct manner. 'Buildings can be built overnight but not great institutions!'

About O2

Fire needs 'oxygen' to burn and spread heat, while we need O2 to 'Energize and Rise'. It was considered as one of the best festivals in the city with the motto "Breathe it! Live it! Love it!" O2 is known to celebrate talent, potential, direct energy and dedication towards something big. It has always enhanced talent and created a sense of responsibility among the youth. We took of on our journey in 2006, and we have come a long way! O2 has scaled heights much beyond everyone's expectations, thus setting exceptional standards. A salute to the enthusiastic young souls! Bhavan's College provides a pre-eminent place to unveil your hidden talent in various fields like PA, LA, FA, Sports, Informals Media & Management. O2 The Fest, Cultural Fest of Bhavan's COllege is back after 8 Years with new energy and enthusiasm. We welcome you to 'O2 THE FEST..... WHERE LIFE BEGINS!'
